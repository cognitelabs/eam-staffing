Fairy Tales are Fake

Dreams don't just "happen." We have to take initiative if we want to land the career that's right for us.

But that process is wraught with endless moments of frustration, irritation, and annoyance.

No wonder more people don't take an active role in managing their career.

But, that's where we come in. We sift through the noise to find you the right opportunities, coach you through the process, and, ultimately land the career of your dreams.

Process Over Pitch

Lots of recruiters will try to "sell" you on different opportunities. Guess you can't blame them, after all your placement is their profit.

But that's not our style.

Our SEARCH process helps you to uncover what matters most to you both personally and professionally, so that you only explore the opportunities best suited to your needs.

A Well-Suited "Fit." Even if Suits Aren't Your Thing.

Finding the right "cultural fit" is important to every job-seeker. And no two people are the same. We take the time to learn about your priorities and find reasons **not** to take a job.

So, if you run out of reasons, you know it's the right one for you.

Earnings Worth Your Time

Nothing is more frustrating than being recruited, feeling like you've found the right opportunity - only to realize the money is all wrong.

We take money off the table early, so we can make sure that each opportunity we present you is worth your time. And your money.
