Our Process

Step 1: Target

We work with our clients to crystalize their requirements. We need to get beyond the cliches like... "We need Sales Rock Star, trusted advisor, who has a great network, understands our industry and knows how to close."

Who doesn't? Our secret sauce allows us to help you fine tune what you are looking for. We focus on knowing and understanding what will make your hire successful after they are in place and we decide who to target.

Step 2: Find

Our partnership with the best Sales Training and Sales Management Consulting company in the world means we understand the ponds, lakes and oceans we fish in. Bottom line, posts don't usually work because the right candidates usually aren't looking. Our recruiters proactively gauge interest with the profiles that match our SEARCH criteria.

Step 3: Assess

Most sales talent interview well. If there is one thing they can sell its themselves. Our team has decades of Sales Leadership experience which allows us to take the screening process to another level. Our extensive screening process results in a detailed assessment of not only the "can do" but the "will do".

Step 4: Hire

Our founders are available play a role in the hiring decision when needed. We've hired >100 sales people with an extremely high success rate. We will provide an external lens that minimizes false positives and maximizes the information needed to make an informed hiring decision.

Step 5: Onboard

We will provide you with an onboarding plan and assist you with the Sales and Management Training elements as needed.

Whatever you're looking for, we can help.

We'll tell you if it's something we can do, or refer you to a trusted partner if we can't.
